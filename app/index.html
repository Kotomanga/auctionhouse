AuctionHouse

Decentralized Asset Auctions

Auctionhouse is a decentralized application (Dapp) that lets users auction off non-fungible on-chain assets. What does this mean? When you have fungible assets like Augur REP tokens, you can easily sell them on an exchange since they're all interchangeable. But when you have a token that represents something one-of-a-kind, something unique, it's better served to auction it off, since there's no liquidity on exchanges for these one off items. Examples of things you can auction include:

  • A domain name in the Ethereum Name Service (auctionhouse.address)
  • An on chain virtual good (the powerful sword in an Ethereum based RPG)
  • A real world good represented by a token on the blockchain (a concert ticket)
  • A large block of tokens sellable at once
  • A token that represents a role in an on-chain economy
  • Read more about what you can auction and how it works in our about page.
